Smart Notifications

Smart notifications are messages that are sent to users based on certain conditions or events. They are designed to make real-time, push-style messaging more relevant, useful, and appropriate for individual users. Smart notifications can be triggered by specific survey answers, custom variables, or custom data from contacts. They can also help to avoid notification overload when a single event causes many notifications at the same time.
